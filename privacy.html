This Privacy Policy governs how Roasted Cream – The Food Cafe ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website or place an order. We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

DPDP Act 2023 Compliant

1. Data We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Order information: Name, phone number, delivery address, order items.
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Razorpay. We never store card/UPI details.
  • Newsletter: Email address (only if you voluntarily subscribe).
  • Reservation details: Name, phone, date/time, party size (for table bookings).
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type (via Google Analytics — anonymised).
  • Cart data: Stored only in your browser's localStorage. Never sent to our servers unless you place an order.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To confirm, prepare, and deliver your food order.
  • To contact you regarding your order status.
  • To send exclusive offers (only if you opted in via newsletter).
  • To improve our website and understand customer preferences (aggregated analytics only).
  • Legal compliance and fraud prevention.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Supabase (Database)

Order and reservation data is stored securely in Supabase (Singapore servers), which is SOC 2 compliant. Data is retained for up to 2 years for business records.

3.2 Razorpay (Payments)

All payment processing is handled by Razorpay Payments Pvt. Ltd., a PCI-DSS certified payment gateway regulated by RBI. Razorpay Privacy Policy →

3.3 Google Analytics 4

We use GA4 for anonymised traffic analytics. IP addresses are anonymised. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

3.4 Google Maps

Location maps are embedded via Google Maps. Google may collect data per their Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Cart data in localStorage (no server cookie; required for checkout).
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics (only if you accept cookies).
  • No advertising cookies are used on this website.

You may decline analytics cookies via our consent banner without affecting your ability to order.

5. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Erase your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for processing at any time.
  • Nominate an individual to exercise your rights on your behalf.

To exercise any right, contact us via WhatsApp at +91 96529 29252 or email (address on file with FSSAI). We will respond within 72 hours.

6. Data Retention

  • Order records: 2 years (for GST and accounting compliance).
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe.
  • Reservation history: 6 months.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymised permanently; raw data 14 months.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS (TLS 1.2+), Row Level Security on our Supabase database, and Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant payment vault. Access to your data is restricted to authorised staff only.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Orders placed for minors must be done by a parent or guardian.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via our website. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
