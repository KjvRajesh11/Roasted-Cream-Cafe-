Our Story

A Warm Hug in the
Form of Food

Born in the heart of East Marredpally, Roasted Cream started with a single dream: to create a space where every bite feels like home. We believe food is more than sustenance — it's a memory, a feeling, a moment shared.

Our chefs pour passion into every dish — from our signature hand-crafted desserts drizzled in dark chocolate to our crispy-edged pizzas baked with patience. We source fresh, choose local, and cook with love.
